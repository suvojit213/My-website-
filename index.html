About Me

My name is Suvojeet Sengupta, an independent singer born on August 1, 2005. I sing predominantly in Hindi and Bengali, and my genres include Indian pop, Indian film pop, and devotional music. My passion for music drives me to create and share melodies that resonate with my audience.
